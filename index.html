photography

I like to capture the moment. Over the last years I did a lot photos. A lot of these are not really valuable but that’s not the point. It’s more like freezing the moment for later. I started with photography when I started riding a BMX. Thanks for that. Today I do it more „professional“ compared to the early days. But it’s just a hobby for me without any intention to earn money with it. It’s just fun and primarily for myself. And of course I love it to have a camera in my hand and play with it. Currently it’s a Fuji x100f Fuji X-Pro2 Fuji X-Pro3 with a 16mm, 35mm and 90mm Lens and a Ricoh GRIIIx for cycling.
